  • Keadaan dimana setelah kepala dilahirkan, bahu anterior tidak dapat lewat dibawah simfisis pubis
  • Kegagalan melahirkan bahu dengan metode biasa
  • Incidence
    • 1 to 2 per 1000 deliveries
    • 16 per 1000 deliveries of babies > 4000 g
  • Diagnosis:
    • Kesulitan melahrikan wajah dan dagu
    • “Turtle Sign”: kepala bayi melekat erat di vulva atau bahkan tertarik kembali
    • Kegagalan paksi luar kepala bayi
    • Kegagalan turunnya bahu

Complications of Shoulder Dystocia

  • Fetal/neonatal
    • death
    • asphyxia and sequelae
    • fractures - clavicle, humerus
    • brachial plexus palsy
  • Maternal
    • postpartum hemorrhage
    • uterine rupture

Faktor Risiko

Risk factors are present in < 50% of cases - post-term pregnancy - maternal obesity - fetal macrosomia - previous shoulder dystocia - operative vaginal delivery - prolonged labour - poorly controlled diabetes

Management of Shoulder Dystocia

  • Ask for help
    • get the mother on your side
    • partner, coach
    • nursing
    • notify physician back up or other appropriate personnel
  • Lift (McRobert's Manuoever) - flexion of thighs on abdomen - requires assistance - 70% of cases are resolved with this manoeuvre alone
    • the buttocks
    • the legs ![[Pasted image 20220906205656.png|300]], ![[Lift the buttocks.png|300]] ![[McRobert's Manuoever.png]]
  • Anterior disimpaction of shoulder
    • rotate to oblique
      • vaginal approach
      • adduction of anterior shoulder by pressure applied to the posterior aspect of the shoulder (the shoulder is pushed toward the chest)
      • consider episiotomy
      • NO fundal pressure ![[Rubin Manoeuver.png|300]]
    • suprapubic pressure (Massanti Manoeuvre)
      • NO fundal pressure
      • Abdominal approach: suprapubic pressure applied with heel of clasped hand from the posterior aspect of the anterior shoulder to dislodge it ![[Manoeuver Massanti.png|300]] ![[Penekan suprapubic.png]]
  • Rotation of the posterior shoulder - Woods’ manoeuver
    • Step 1
      • pressure on anterior aspect of posterior shoulder
      • may be combined with anterior disimpaction manoeuvers
      • NO fundal pressure ![[Manuver wood 1.png|300]]
    • Step 2
      • Wood’s screw manoeuvre
      • can be done simultaneously with anterior dissimpaction ![[Manuver wood 2.png|300]] Step 3
      • may be repeated if delivery not accomplished by Steps 1 & 2 ![[Manuver wood 3.png|300]]
    • Step 4 ![[Manuver wood 4.png|300]]
  • Manual removal of posterior arm
    • flex arm at elbow
    • (pressure in antecubital fossa to flex arm)
    • sweep arm over chest
    • grasp wrist/forearm or hand
    • deliver arm ![[Manual removal of posterior arm.png|300]] ![[Manual removal of posterior arm 2.png|300]] ![[Algoritma penanganan distosia bahu.png|450]] Avoid the P’s
  • Panic
  • Pulling (on the head)
  • Pushing (on the fundus)
  • Pivoting (sharply angulating the head, using the coccyx as a fulcrum)
